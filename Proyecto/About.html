About Carrer





Description of career


The Software Engineering program at the National Technical University, as highly trained professionals to develop world-class software. Using a unique methodology, proven, achieves an ideal balance between theory and practice, emulating real situations to achieve wide acceptance in the labor market. Our curriculum prepares engineers who can work on different facets of the process of software development and evolution, such as: needs investigation, analysis, specification, design, programming, database modeling, documentation, integration, assurance quality and implementation. In our program we study techniques and technologies that have application in domestic and international markets: development processes, object-oriented languages​​, component models, relational databases and SQL language, object modeling with UML, programming for the Internet / Web and mobile devices, use and application of standards, teamwork, among others.



Professional Field


The graduate of this race will be prepared to work individually or in teams, providing engineering vision and professionalism, in works such as:


  • Designer and programmer databases.
  • Specifier Analyst and software systems.
  • Scheduler applications and components for mobile devices.
  • Designer software systems on various technology platforms.
  • Scheduler components in high-level languages ​​and low-level.
  • Scheduler software applications and services on the Web/Internet.
  • Designer and builder applications that use collaborative technologies.
  • Applications programmer high-level languages ​​for information management, business, or entertainment.


Student profile


Candidate studying Software Engineering must have aptitude for mathematical and logical reasoning to solve problems. You must also have an interest in information technology and the development of computer programs. Furthermore, it is desirable that the student has a positive attitude toward learning and continuous improvement and teamwork.
